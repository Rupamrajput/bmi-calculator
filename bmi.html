Body Mass Index

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of the amount of fat in a human body. It is a single numeric value with the following interpretation:

Underweight Less than 18
Normal 18-25
Overweight 25-30
Over 30 More than 30

Use the calculator below to calculate your BMI.

Note:
For metric measurements, use centimeters for height and kilograms for weight.
For imperial measurements, use foot and inches for height and pounds for weight.
